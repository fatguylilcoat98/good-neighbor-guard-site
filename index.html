How It Works

What happens when you ask a question

Behind every answer is a process built to protect you. Here's what Veracore does — in plain language.

Step 01 — Compute, Don't Guess
If it can be calculated, it is.
Math, dates, and known facts are answered with certainty. No AI guessing — a calculator cannot hallucinate.
Step 02 — Ask Everyone Independently
Four AI systems research separately.
Each one finds its own sources. They can't copy each other. This is how we catch the ones that made something up.
Step 03 — Try to Prove It Wrong
One AI's only job is to find the flaw.
Before you see anything, a dedicated system attacks the answer looking for errors, gaps, and wrong assumptions.
