This is a scam. The IRS never calls to demand immediate payment or threaten arrest. Hang up immediately and do not give any money or personal information.

The real IRS only contacts people by mail first. They never demand gift cards, wire transfers, or cryptocurrency. They never threaten immediate arrest. If you're unsure, call the IRS directly at 1-800-829-1040. You can also report the call at reportfraud.ftc.gov — this protects other people from the same scam.
